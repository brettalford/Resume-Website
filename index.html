Who am I?

I've been using technology since before I can remember, before I could type, before I could even spell properly. In some of my earliest experiences with technology I would have my mom or dad or sister or even babysitter log me on to play games or watch videos and I loved it. At the time I had no idea about how these things worked, what went on in the background or how a computer could be used to make the world better I just knew that it was cool and that i wanted to take it apart and see how it functioned. Technology makes an impact, software and the hardware that goes with it has changed my life in so many ways and I want to be a part of making something that can make an impact on other people too. I enjoy problem solving, there's not much that I find more satisfying than using all of the skills at my disposal and finally finishing something that I had worked long and hard on and actually having a result that I worked hard on to bring into reality.
